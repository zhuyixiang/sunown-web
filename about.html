About Us

Providing The Highest Quality
Products and Services

THE SUN OWNER operates as a specialty ingredients distributor, catering to the food, beverage, nutraceutical, pharmaceutical, and cosmeceutical industries with top-notch ingredients. Our rigorous vendor qualification process ensures that all manufacturers are GMP-certified.

Our extensive experience and accomplishments have established us as the go-to solution for industry manufacturers.

Our commitment to quality is reflected in sourcing ingredients solely from audited and inspected producers worldwide. With a dedicated focus on quality control and documentation, THE SUN OWNER continually seeks out and procures new and innovative ingredients for the US marketplace.

We have assisted suppliers worldwide in implementing the necessary processes and procedures required by auditing agencies to successfully supply materials to consumers.

We firmly believe that superior customer relationships and quality products are essential for a thriving business. Upholding these principles, we continue to expand and serve our customers diligently.

Customer service remains our top priority, and we consistently deliver excellent service.

Sourcing the Highest
Quality Ingredients

At THE SUN OWNER, sourcing top-quality ingredients is not just a priority, it's a commitment we take seriously. We understand that the success of our clients depends on the excellence of the ingredients they use, so we leave no stone unturned in our search for the finest products. Our rigorous sourcing process involves building strong relationships with trusted suppliers worldwide, meticulously vetting each ingredient for quality, sustainability, and ethical production practices. We prioritize transparency, ensuring that our clients know exactly where their ingredients come from and how they are sourced. By staying informed about the latest industry trends and innovations, we ensure that we always offer a diverse selection of premium ingredients to meet the evolving needs of our clients.
